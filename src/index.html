This site will help you with studying such a wonderful topic as Algorithms! This site contains the basic sorting , search and optimization algorithms. When I first started studying algorithms, it was difficult for me to figure out what they were for and where to use them in general. It was only after a long time that I was able to solve the most primitive Medium tasks on Leetcode . That's why I created this site to make it easier for novice programmers to enter such an amazing and beautiful world of algorithms. Have fun learning, if it gets really difficult, click on the Penguin, it will help!
